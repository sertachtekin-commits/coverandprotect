# Legal Review Required

The legal and compliance pages in this branch are business-ready drafts, not legal advice.

Before relying on them for regulated insurance advertising, review the pages for:

- Correct FSRA licence number
- Correct business identity
- Correct contact information
- Correct insurer relationship disclosures
- Correct refund/cancellation wording for the insurers you actually use
- Compliance with current insurer, MGA, FSRA, privacy, and advertising requirements

Do not use advertising language that promises guaranteed approval, guaranteed lowest price, government affiliation, or active coverage before insurer acceptance.

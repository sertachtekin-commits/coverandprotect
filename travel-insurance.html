Super Visa Insurance
For Parents & Grandparents

Bringing your parents or grandparents to Canada on a Super Visa? Canadian immigration requires proof of private medical insurance from a Canadian insurer — valid for a minimum of one year, with at least $100,000 in coverage. We specialize in finding the most affordable, IRCC-compliant Super Visa plans available.

📋 IRCC Super Visa Requirements
  • Minimum $100,000 coverage for health care, hospitalization & repatriation
  • Valid for at least 1 year from the date of entry
  • Must be from a Canadian insurance company
  • Must be valid for each entry to Canada
  • Proof of purchase required with Super Visa application

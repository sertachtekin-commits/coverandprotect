Visitor Insurance Toronto

Visitor insurance can help protect visitors to Toronto and Canada from unexpected emergency medical expenses.

Common situations

Visitors may include parents visiting family, tourists, new immigrants waiting for provincial coverage, returning Canadians, and temporary residents.

We compare coverage amount, deductible, trip length, pre-existing condition wording, refund rules, and insurer claim support.

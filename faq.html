Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about quotes, licensing, coverage, privacy, and the insurance process.

Are consultations free?

Yes. Initial consultations and quote requests are free unless a separate written fee arrangement is made in advance.

Is Cover & Protect licensed?

Yes. Cover & Protect is operated by Sertac Tekin, an Ontario-licensed insurance advisor regulated by FSRA. Licence #10112782.

Which insurance companies do you work with?

As an independent advisor, Cover & Protect may compare options from multiple Canadian insurers and insurance providers. Availability depends on product type, eligibility, underwriting, and insurer rules.

Does a quote mean I am covered?

No. A quote is not an insurance contract. Coverage is not active until the insurer accepts the application, confirms the effective date, and receives any required payment.

How long does it take to get a quote?

Many quote requests can be reviewed within 24 hours, depending on the product, applicant details, insurer systems, and whether additional information is required.

How is my personal information protected?

Your information is used to respond to requests, prepare quotes, compare insurance options, and communicate about insurance services. Cover & Protect does not sell personal information. See the Privacy Policy.

Can I cancel a policy?

Cancellation and refund rules depend on the issuing insurer and policy wording. See the Refund & Cancellation Policy.

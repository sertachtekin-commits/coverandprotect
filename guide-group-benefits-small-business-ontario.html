Group Benefits for Ontario Small Businesses

A group benefits plan can help small businesses attract and retain employees while giving staff practical protection for health, dental, disability, and life coverage.

Plan availability, pricing, renewals, participation requirements, and coverage terms are determined by the insurer. Cover & Protect helps compare options and explain trade-offs.

What a group plan can include

Common benefits include health, dental, prescription drugs, paramedical services, vision, life insurance, accidental death, short-term disability, long-term disability, and employee assistance programs.

Why small businesses use group benefits

Benefits support retention, recruitment, employee morale, and compensation competitiveness. For many employees, predictable health and dental coverage is more useful than a small wage increase.

Cost control

Cost can be managed with plan design, co-insurance, deductibles, maximums, benefit classes, health spending accounts, pooled benefits, and renewal review.

Common mistakes

Common mistakes include buying a plan without reviewing employee demographics, ignoring renewal risk, underestimating disability coverage, or choosing the cheapest plan without reviewing claim limits.

How Cover & Protect helps

We review business size, employee needs, budget, existing coverage, and plan objectives before comparing available insurer options.

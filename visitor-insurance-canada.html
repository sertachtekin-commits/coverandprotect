Visitor insurance questions

Is visitor insurance required?

It depends on the visitor’s status and purpose, but emergency medical coverage is strongly recommended because visitors are not covered by provincial health insurance — see our guide Does OHIP cover visitors to Canada?

Can visitors with pre-existing conditions apply?

Often yes. The right option depends on the condition, stability period, age and insurer rules. Ask Cover & Protect if you are not sure.

How much coverage should we choose?

Families often compare different coverage amounts and deductibles. The right balance depends on risk comfort, visitor age, travel dates and budget.

Can I apply online?

Yes. Secure TruStone Health online links are available above. If you are unsure which one fits, request a quote or call before applying.

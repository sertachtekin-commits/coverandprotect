## Compliance notes

Avoid:
- Guaranteed approval
- Guaranteed lowest price
- Best price in Canada
- Instant coverage if not actually instant
- Misleading government affiliation

Use:
- Free consultation
- Licensed Ontario advisor
- Quotes subject to underwriting
- Coverage subject to insurer terms

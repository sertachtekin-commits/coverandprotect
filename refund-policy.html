Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy explains how refunds and cancellations are handled for insurance advisory services and insurance policies.

Insurance policies are issued by third-party insurers. Refunds, cancellations, free-look periods, and administrative charges are governed by the issuing insurer's policy wording and rules.

1. Advisory Consultations

Initial consultations and quote requests through Cover & Protect are generally provided at no charge unless a separate written fee agreement is made in advance.

2. Insurance Premiums

Premiums are paid to or processed for the issuing insurer or its authorized administrator. Cover & Protect does not unilaterally determine insurer refund amounts, cancellation charges, payment schedules, or eligibility for refund.

3. Cancellation Requests

If you wish to cancel a policy, contact us and we can guide you through the applicable insurer process. The insurer may require written confirmation, signed forms, policy details, proof of non-travel, visa refusal letters, or other documents.

4. Travel, Visitor, and Super Visa Insurance

Refund rules for travel, visitor to Canada, and Super Visa insurance vary by insurer. Some policies may allow a full or partial refund before the effective date if travel has not started. Some Super Visa policies may provide a refund if the visa is refused and required documents are submitted. Terms vary by insurer.

5. Life, Health, Critical Illness, Disability, and Group Benefits

Cancellation and refund treatment for life, health, critical illness, disability, and group benefits policies depends on the insurer, product type, billing cycle, policy issue date, statutory rights, and policy wording.

6. No Guarantee of Refund

We cannot guarantee that an insurer will approve a refund or cancellation request. Any refund is subject to the insurer's rules, policy terms, timing, and any applicable fees or earned premium.

7. How to Request Assistance

To request cancellation or refund assistance, email contact@coverandprotect.ca or call 647-366-9495. Include your name, policy type, insurer, policy number if available, reason for cancellation, and requested effective date.

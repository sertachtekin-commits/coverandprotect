# Google Ads Launch Plan - Cover & Protect

## Campaign structure

Recommended Search campaigns:

1. Super Visa Insurance
   - super visa insurance ontario
   - super visa insurance toronto
   - parent super visa insurance
   - super visa insurance quote

2. Visitor to Canada Insurance
   - visitor insurance canada
   - visitor to canada insurance quote
   - insurance for visitors to canada

3. Travel Insurance
   - travel insurance ontario
   - travel medical insurance
   - snowbird travel insurance

4. Health Insurance
   - personal health insurance ontario
   - family health insurance ontario
   - dental and health insurance ontario

5. Group Benefits
   - group benefits for small business
   - employee benefits plan ontario
   - small business health benefits

## Negative keywords

Add negatives early:

- jobs
- career
- salary
- free insurance
- government insurance
- OHIP application
- scam
- meaning
- pdf
- sample
- template
- login
- claim form
- phone number only

## Landing page checks

Each landing page should include:

- Clear headline matching ad group
- FSRA licence disclosure
- Phone number
- Email/contact form
- Privacy link
- Insurance disclaimer link
- No misleading guarantee language
- Strong CTA above the fold

## Conversion tracking

Track:

- Quote form submit
- Contact form submit
- Phone click
- Calendly click
- Thank-you page visit

## Budget recommendation

Start focused, not broad. For early testing, prioritize Super Visa, Visitor to Canada, and Group Benefits before expanding to broad life/health keywords.

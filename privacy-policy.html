Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Cover & Protect collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information submitted through this website, lead forms, phone calls, email, booking links, chat features, advertising campaigns, and related communications.

Contact: contact@coverandprotect.ca · 647-366-9495 · Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

We do not sell personal information. Information submitted through this site is used to respond to requests, prepare insurance quotes, provide advisory support, and communicate about insurance-related services.

We may collect the information you voluntarily provide, including name, phone number, email address, insurance type requested, destination, travel dates, age or age range, number of travellers or employees, business name, current coverage details, health-related underwriting information you choose to provide, and other details needed to respond to your request.

  • To respond to quote requests, consultation bookings, and contact forms.
  • To compare insurance options from Canadian insurers and prepare recommendations.
  • To communicate by phone, email, SMS, or calendar invite when you request assistance.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the website.
  • To measure advertising performance and understand which campaigns generate inquiries.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, insurer, and licensing obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share information with insurers, managing general agencies, underwriting platforms, quoting systems, compliance providers, website hosting providers, form processors, analytics providers, calendar providers, and other service providers only as reasonably required to provide services, operate the website, or comply with obligations.

Insurance products are underwritten by third-party insurers. Any information sent to insurers or underwriting platforms may be subject to their own privacy policies and underwriting rules.

This website may use cookies, Google Analytics, Google Ads conversion tracking, remarketing tags, form tracking, and similar technologies. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No online system is completely secure. We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for service, compliance, dispute resolution, record keeping, and legitimate business purposes.

7. Access, Correction, and Deletion Requests

You may request access to your personal information, ask for correction, or request deletion where legally permitted. Some information may need to be retained for compliance, insurance, legal, or record-keeping purposes.

8. Children

This website is intended for adults seeking insurance information and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

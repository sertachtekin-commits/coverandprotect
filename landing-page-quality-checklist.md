# Landing Page Quality Checklist

Use this before launching each ad group.

## Above the fold

- Headline matches the ad group keyword.
- CTA is visible without scrolling.
- Phone number is visible.
- Service area is clear: Ontario / GTA / Toronto.
- FSRA licence disclosure is visible or one click away.

## Trust

- Licensed advisor disclosure.
- Independent advisor positioning.
- Insurer names used carefully, without implying false endorsement.
- Reviews or trust signals visible.
- Legal pages linked in footer.

## Form

- Short form.
- Privacy consent visible.
- Privacy Policy linked.
- Thank-you page or confirmation works.
- Mobile form is easy to use.

## Compliance

- No guaranteed approval.
- No guaranteed lowest price.
- No misleading government affiliation.
- No coverage promise before insurer acceptance.
- No unsupported claim that a product is best for everyone.

## Technical

- Page loads on mobile.
- Buttons work.
- Phone links work.
- Sitemap includes page.
- Canonical URL is correct.
- GA4 and Google Ads conversion tracking active.
